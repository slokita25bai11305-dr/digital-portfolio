Part B – Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:




Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students


	Do's ✅
	

	Don'ts ❌
	1
	Think before you post — ask if you'd be comfortable with a professor or employer seeing it
	1
	Don't post offensive, hateful, or discriminatory content
	2
	Use privacy settings and review them regularly
	2
	Don't share personal details like your address, ID, or daily location
	3
	Fact-check information before sharing or reposting
	3
	Don't vent about classmates, professors, or workplaces publicly
	4
	Give credit when sharing others' ideas, images, or quotes
	4
	Don't share exam answers, assignments, or engage in academic dishonesty online
	5
	Ask permission before tagging or posting photos of others
	5
	Don't respond to trolls or online arguments — report and disengage
	6
	Keep your profile and username professional
	6
	Don't ignore cyberbullying — reporting it is the responsible choice
	7
	Engage positively — support peers and contribute constructively
	7
	Don't treat public platforms like private conversations
